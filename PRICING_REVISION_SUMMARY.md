# Pricing Page Revision Summary

**Date:** 2025-01-27  
**Status:** ✅ Complete Revision Guide Created

---

## 🎯 What Changed

### 1. **Removed Overselling & Unsubstantiated Claims**

**Removed:**
- ❌ "Worth $300+ in consultant fees" - Can't prove this
- ❌ "Save 18+ hours/month" - Can't guarantee this
- ❌ "Professional-grade analysis" - Subjective claim
- ❌ "70% cheaper than competitors" - Requires constant monitoring
- ❌ ROI calculations - Unsubstantiated

### 2. **Removed Legal/Formal Language**

**Removed:**
- ❌ "Professional oversight"
- ❌ "Expert-curated"
- ❌ "Managed by cybersecurity and legal professionals"
- ❌ "Guaranteed response time"
- ❌ Any language that sounds like a legal commitment

### 3. **Made Language User-Friendly**

**Changed:**
- "Choose Your Privacy Protection Plan" → "Pick Your Plan"
- "Essential privacy features" → "Everything you need to start protecting your privacy"
- "Advanced features and unlimited usage" → "Unlimited assessments, detailed analysis, and all the free features"

### 4. **Clarified How Support Actually Works**

**Before:**
```
"Priority email support - Faster response for security issues"
```

**After:**
```
"Email Support - Contact us by email if you need help. We'll respond as quickly as we can"
```

**Added FAQ:**
- "How does email support work?" - Explains it's just email, no dedicated team, response times vary

### 5. **Clarified How Service Catalog Actually Works**

**Before:**
```
"Professional management - Service catalog managed by experts"
```

**After:**
```
"Updated Catalog - Our service catalog is regularly updated with the latest privacy information"
```

**Added FAQ:**
- "How is the service catalog updated?" - Explains it's curated data from public sources, not real-time expert management
