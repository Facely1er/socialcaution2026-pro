## 🎯 **Calendar Features in SocialCaution:**

### **Personalization Engine:**
- **Risk-based prioritization** - High-risk areas first
- **Account-specific actions** - Based on your actual digital footprint
- **Difficulty scaling** - Easy wins first, advanced measures later
- **Time-appropriate tasks** - Seasonal relevance (tax season, holiday shopping, etc.)

### **Progress Tracking:**
- **Monthly privacy score updates**
- **Completion tracking with visual progress**
- **Impact measurement** - see what actions helped most
- **Adaptive recommendations** - calendar adjusts based on progress

### **Integration Features:**
- **Calendar app sync** - Add to Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar
- **Reminder notifications** - Weekly task reminders
- **Achievement tracking** - Celebrate completed months
- **Sharing options** - Family privacy calendar coordination

### **Business Value:**
- **Corporate calendars** - Team or department privacy improvement
- **Compliance tracking** - Meet quarterly privacy requirements
- **Executive reporting** - Show organizational privacy posture improvement
- **Training integration** - Connect calendar to security awareness programs
